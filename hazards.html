Beware of those which your kid’s should be aware of

We are here to help you. As today's kid’s face a very different set of challenges with a whole new set of online experiences, they need parent’s assistance to get through it safely. Let us help them together.

let’s start from the problem

Consider the internet world to be a reflection of the real world; children might be at risk both in the real and online worlds. However, in the internet world, parents may have little understanding of the risks their children face - the reality is that child online abuse exists and can manifest itself in a variety of ways.

The Parents

should be aware of the problems by over use of digital devices for their kids. These are the core problems it creates for the child eventually.

Lower Attention Span

Excessive usage of the most prevalent modern gadgets, such as computers, cellphones, and tablets, can lead to distraction and focus problems. It can eventually affects their academic success.

Minimized Social Interaction

The internet has a negative impact on a child's social abilities when they play too many computer games and get disconnected from real life. If a kid does not communicate, interact, or share with his or her environment, he or she will try to meet all of these demands in a virtual space.

Physical Health Problems

Technology's negative effects on children are not restricted to emotional and mental illnesses. The subject has a physical dimension as well. It might happen as a result of watching TV for an extended period of time, using a computer or tablet in an incorrect seating position, and so on.

Reduced Quality of Sleep

Children, as well as adults, can suffer from sleeping disorders by overuse of internet or related technologies. They used to spend more time late nights and waking up too late. Exposure of hard lights before sleep is also affecting sleep quality.

Risk of Depression

Too much time spent on video games, smartphones, and watching television has been related to increased levels of anxiety and sadness in youngsters. Increased use of social media, on the other hand, raises feelings of inadequacy, discontent, and isolation. As a result, these feelings have a severe impact on your mood and worsen symptoms of despair, anxiety, and tension.

The digital threats

aginst children as increasing day by day. As a parent you should have sufficent knowledge about the digital threats your kid may face while he or she is using digital media. We can’t say our kid’s not to go online or interact online, instead we can make them aware of the problems they may encounter.

Online predators are continuously on the lookout for new ways to abuse children, and the more youngsters that use technology, the higher the risk. They may create, access, or share online sexual abuse materials involving children, and/or attempt to groom them for sexual exploitation. Predators use technology as a means of achieving their objectives and will be aware of the vulnerabilities they might use to obtain access to youngsters online. These are some of the major online threats your child may encounter when going online.

Cyberbullying

When someone bullies your kids using electronic methods, this is referred to as cyberbullying. This could include internet-based social media and messaging services accessed via a mobile phone, tablet.
