Not only Threats and Hazards

Know about advantages of online activity for your kid

Not only Hazrds and threats there are numerous advantages also for a child who uses technology in a positive way. There is a lot of research being done on child technology use, and the results are mixed. Some experts say that young children should not be exposed to screens at all, while others say that in the right environment, and when combined with other activities like reading and exercise, it can be beneficial.

Physical activity, reading, and human connection will always play a vital role in child development, and personal computing should not replace or detract from these experiences; yet, in the correct setting, technology can provide new educational experiences to young children, helping them develop their talents and extend their horizons:

The Conversation guide

When you talk openly with your children not only increases their knowledge of the issue, but also their willingness to tell you when problems arise. These conversation starters and discussion guides can assist in guiding difficult but necessary conversations.

First steps

How to start talking...

Always keep in mind that a conversation starter is just that - a means to start a conversation. And, if you can sense how your kid is feeling, the manner in which you open a conversation can go a long way. A ridiculous question in a funny voice, an honest question with a soft touch, or a positive question with an enthusiastic tone, for example, can all be suitable and productive approaches to dive deep and gain a better knowledge of your child's mental state.
